Additional information

Tuition fees at Middlesex University London

Students who are citizens of the EU/EEA:

The average tuition fees for students from the EU/EEA are 9,600 GBP per year, ranging from 4,100 GBP to 19,400 GBP.

Students who are citizens from outside the EU/EEA:

The average tuition fees for students from outside the EU/EEA are 13,700 GBP per year, ranging from 4,800 GBP to 24,300 GBP.

Tuition fees may vary for each study programme.

English requirements

When you apply to Middlesex University London, you will need to prove your English proficiency.

  • This university accepts IELTS. The general minimum IELTS score is 6.0 overall for Bachelors degrees and 6.5 overall for Masters degrees.
  • This university accepts TOEFL iBT. The general minimum TOEFL score is 72 overall for Bachelors degrees and 87 overall for Masters degrees. The TOEFL institution code (DI Code) for Middlesex University London is: 9719.
  • This university accepts Cambridge English certificates (CAE/CPE). The general minimum score is 169 overall for Bachelors degrees and 176 overall for Masters degrees.
  • This university accepts the Pearson Test of English (PTE). The general minimum score is 51 overall for Bachelors degrees and 58 overall for Masters degrees.

Please note that the required English scores for admission may vary for certain programmes.
