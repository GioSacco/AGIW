Best Flipbook Software to Consider

  in Research   Posted on December 22, 2020

While there are a lot of digital formats for printed media like .pdf, .doc, ePub, iPDF, and more, a Flipbook is arguably the closest one to give a “book-like” experience. It faithfully represents its source material from cover to cover with pages arranged exactly as they are in real life. The most notable part of the experience is the flipping animation that simulates a real page being turned.

However, this type of software is not just about the realistic reading experience because it also helps in learning a reading material. A flipbook is a type of multimedia tool developed for textbooks, which provides ease of reading and learning without the need to carry thick books (Prasetyono & Hariyono, 2020). Furthermore, Sugianto et al. (2013) found that the virtual module based on Flipbooks has an impact on improving learning outcomes, especially in learning digital basics.

There are mainly three types of Flipbook software, namely desktop, web-based, and a combination of both solutions. Their pricing varies as well, ranging from free to a monthly subscription or a one-time payment. Therefore, finding a Flipbook tool suited to your needs might prove to be quite a task and this article seeks to help you do just that. This fairly short read provides a list of the best Flipbook solutions in the market today together with their pros, cons, and pricing plans to help you with your research.

The advent of the internet has revolutionized the way information is disseminated and one of the most affected channels is print media. Queues in the libraries have become shorter, newspapers have fewer subscribers, fliers are less common, etc. However, this is not to say that print media is dying. There are still a few ways that print media outshines its digital counterparts such as having less competition for ads, being a more reliable source of information, being better suited for kinesthetic learning, and more.

With that said, there are also many advantages in opting for digital media, including having access to information right from your smartphone, tablet, or computer. Digital media is also a big player when it comes to advertisements because it can be created, launched, and updated faster. Add to that the interactive capability of digital media and its ability to precisely target a suitable audience. Its effectivity is also evident by the steady rise of ePublishing revenue in the years to come (Lindlahr, 2020).

Source: Digital Market Outlook, 2020

That is why a lot of information that is used to be available only in print has started moving to a digital stage as well. Ebooks, online news, and online encyclopedias are a few examples. Some try to bring the best of both worlds—print and digital—into life. And one of them is the flipbook format. It makes the source material accessible through digital devices like phones and laptops while making the reading experience a little more grounded on reality. This is made possible through flipbook software that converts a source material into a flipbook format and makes them available to read. Here are some of them.

Flipbook Software Solutions

1. PUBHTML5

PUBHTML5 is one of the best flipbook software you can find in the market these days. This digital publishing platform is cloud-based so anyone across the globe will be able to enjoy the service. Furthermore, it makes the platform more reliable and secure. The solution seamlessly works on multiple devices, including Windows, Mac, Android, and iOS.

In terms of features, the platform offers a fair number of functionalities that make it possible to turn PDF, OpenOffice, and MS Office into eye-catching flipbooks. In its free version, users have access to multiple pre-designed templates. However, the service embeds a company watermark into them so it is not ideal for corporate use. If you wish to create a flipbook with no watermark, you should opt for paid versions.  The service offers three different pricing plans ranging from $12.50/month to $83.50/month.

Flipbooks created using this tool are saved in the cloud, enabling users to access them anytime, and anywhere. Your readers can view your digital publication on their desktop whenever and wherever they want to. Also, you can save your work on a USB drive, CD/DVD, HTML5, or ZIP.

Pros

  • Free version includes pre-designed templates
  • Free cloud storage
  • Supports multiple devices

Cons

  • Free trial does not allow offline viewing

2. iSpring Flip

iSpring Flip, a part of iSpring suite of solutions, is another flip book maker solution that lets users turn their documents into engaging materials. iSpring includes simple learning management system integrations, is easy to use, and has impressive reviews (Landay, 2010 cited in Matsuda et al. 2017). Its modalities are groundbreaking tools to create engaging learning platforms (Lewis-Pierre & Aziza, 2017).

The platform supports a number of popular formats including Word, PDF, and PowerPoint. You can convert any of these files into interactive HTML5 books compatible with modern elearning delivery standards such as SCORM, cmi5, AICC, and xAPI. What is more, you can upload the flipbook to an LMS, share it with your readers, and even track what they are reading.

iSpring Flip offers a reasonable pricing plan. Unfortunately, no free version is available. But they do provide a free trial that lasts for 14 days and the user can check out all the features that the platform offers.

Pros

  • Supports popular file formats
  • Compatible with all modern elearning standards
  • User-friendly
  • Track what readers are reading

Cons

  • Does not offer a free version
  • No built-in templates available

3. Flipsnack

Flipsnack is another tool that can be used to publish digital content. This tool helps you create a flash-based flipbook from a pdf file with ease. The free version allows you to create a flipbook with up to three pdf files, each having up to fifteen pages (Troutner, 2011).

The platform is web-based so there is no need for any installation. Its user interface is highly intuitive so anyone can get a hang of the solution in no time. A vast collection of templates are also available, and you can access them even on the free version. This solution basically delivers the tool needed to create professional and visually appealing flipbooks. Also, it supports multiple sharing options. You can download your flipbook via HTML5 or as a PDF, embed it on your website, share it via email, or share its link on any social network.

In terms of pricing plans, the service offers three paid packages in addition to its free version. They include advanced functionalities that are not accessible on the free version.

Pros

  • Offers a free version that lets you create a flipbook with multiple PDF files
  • A vast array of pre-built templates, all can be used free of charge
  • Easily embed flipbooks on your website or blog
  • Comes with network sharing capability

Cons

  • The free version lets you create a flipbook with only up to 30 pages coming from a maximum of three PDF files
  • Some advanced features cannot be accessed on the free version

4. Flip HTML5

Flip HTML5 is just like PUBHTML5 as it delivers a wide array of functionalities that lets users turn PDF files into visually-appealing digital publications. And just like most of the software on this list, Flip HTML5 can also be used for free. The free version allows users to create a book with up to 500 pages. However, it does include a pre-designed template so you have to start from scratch. Templates are only available in their paid plans. On the good side, the platform allows its users to store their publications online for free. So you do not need to have a website to be able to publish your work. It also supports multiple languages, including Japanese, German, Spanish, etc.

Furthermore, the software helps you generate income from your flipbooks with less effort. Thanks to its ecommerce feature, you can sell your books with ease. There are other interesting features such as an online editor that enables users to add multimedia to their digital publication even without installing the desktop client. However, most of them are only accessible on higher-tier premium accounts.

For their paid plans, Flip HTML5 pricing starts at $12.5/month and can go as high as $83.5/month.

Pros

  • Free-version available
  • Supports multiple languages
  • Free online hosting

Cons

  • Must have higher-tier premium accounts to use several interactive functions, including templates

5. Yumpu

Yumpu is another web-based software that can be used to easily convert PDF into flipbooks. Just like the other software on this list, Yumpu also offers a free version. And it comes with extensive functionalities. One of which is the ability to easily integrate your flipbook into your website or blog.

There is also the AdFree version. As the name implies, third-party advertisements will not be shown in and around your publication. Meaning to say, there will be no distractions and your readers can remain focused reading your work. It costs 1$19.99/month, which is pretty reasonable, considering you will enjoy the aforementioned benefit and a plethora of other advanced functionality that it offers.

In addition to free and AdFree, Yumpu also offers other products WEBKiosk, APPKiosk, and PROKiosk. Generally, the price range of this software is $0 – $219/mo. The pricing for the latter package, however, is available upon request.

Pros

  • AdFree version guarantees that there will be no third-party advertisements on your publication
  • Also comes with a free version

Cons

  • You need to subscribe to the paid version to access advanced functionalities such as Google Analytics

Find the Right Flipbook Option for You

There you have it, a concise list of some of the best flipbook solutions out there. As you might have noticed, most of them promote, more or less, the same set of advantages but upon closer inspection reveals crucial differences. These include prices, hosting services, conversion capacity, and more.

Furthermore, there are a lot more options for you out there in case you want to expand your search radius. So, to make sure that you find the flipbook solution that will fully fulfill your needs, researching other similar tools is encouraged.
